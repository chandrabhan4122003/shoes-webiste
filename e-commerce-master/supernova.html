SUPERNOVA

Roll through your next training run with confidence in a pair of the new Supernova shoes from adidas. Designed for runners of all types who are looking to get the most out of their miles, adidas Supernova sneakers feature Dreamstrike+ technology, the midsole is cushioned with re-engineered super foam. Built to help first-time racers prepare to run their best.

ADIDAS SUPERNOVA: YEAR-ROUND COMFORT

The enhanced comfort of the adidas Supernova range allows you to create a stylish running wardrobe that will sustain your motivation and keep you training all year round. The clean lines of Supernova running shoes and clothes for men and women look and feel great in all weathers. Casual styling works with technical fabrics to free your mind so you can keep pushing through, even during the colder months. Water repellent, breathable designs keep your enthusiasm sharp, so that heading out of the door feels like a pleasure and not a chore, at any time of year. Reflective, sweat-wicking technologies worked into irresistible adidas designs guarantee you not only look sharp, but you feel good when running in colder and wet conditions.

SUPERNOVA+ SHOES: YOUR BEST FOOT FORWARD

The adidas Supernova+ is a neutral running shoe designed for people starting out on their running journey. adidas Supernova Boost technology in these comfortable running shoes is a gift that keeps on giving, delivering outstanding energy return to keep your feet nimble and fresh as your running distances increase. Suitable for shorter jogs and longer distances, features include enhanced support and cushioning for perfect foot position, with additional Boost technology in the forefoot to put a noticeable spring in your step, so your feet can go further, for longer. Constructed from high-performance Primegreen recycled materials, these running shoes are breathable for flexibility and support.

CLEAN DESIGNS AND COMFORT

A truly modern range that is bursting with easy-to-wear pieces that combine comfort and relaxed adidas styling. These lightweight, well-cut designs are ideal to throw on run after run.
